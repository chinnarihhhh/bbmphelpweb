About Us

This website was created with the goal of bridging the gap between individuals in need and NGOs operating within the BBMP jurisdiction in Bengaluru. Witnessing the struggles of vulnerable communities, I realized the need for a simple platform where people could request help easily and directly. Through a straightforward help form, this website connects users with NGOs that can provide support, whether it’s for shelter, food, medical aid, or other necessities. My aim is to ensure that assistance reaches those who need it most, fostering a community where no one is left behind.

Shelter Finder

Looking for a safe place to stay? Our Shelter Finder tool connects you to various shelters and safe spaces in the BBMP jurisdiction. Here’s how it works: Easy Search: Simply enter your location and the type of shelter you need (e.g., temporary shelter, long-term housing). List of Shelters: The tool will provide a list of nearby shelters with essential details such as location, contact information, and available services. Contact NGOs: If you're unable to find a shelter or need immediate assistance, the website can direct your request to an NGO that specializes in housing support. This service aims to help individuals and families quickly find a safe place, ensuring that no one is left without shelter.

Our Impact

The impact of this platform is simple but significant. By connecting individuals in need with NGOs and shelters in the BBMP jurisdiction, we aim to provide timely assistance and support to those facing homelessness, food insecurity, and other challenges. This website serves as a bridge, simplifying access to vital resources and ensuring that help reaches the right people at the right time. Whether it’s connecting someone to a shelter, offering medical aid, or providing information about available resources, the goal is to create a community where no one is left behind. Every request submitted has the potential to change a life, making Bengaluru a more caring and supportive city for everyone.

How You Can Help

You can make a difference in the lives of those who need it most. Here’s how you can get involved: Volunteer: Join hands with local NGOs and offer your time and skills to help those in need. Whether it’s helping with outreach, distributing supplies, or providing support at shelters, every little effort counts. Donate: Support the cause by donating funds, food, clothes, or other resources. Your donations can directly help NGOs provide essential services to those facing hardship. Spread the Word: Share this platform with your friends, family, and community. The more people who know about it, the more individuals in need can be helped. Awareness is key to creating lasting change.
